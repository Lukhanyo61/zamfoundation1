When you [...] meet with another honest Man in similar Distress, you must pay me by lending this Sum to him; enjoining him to discharge the Debt by a like operation, when he shall be able, and shall meet with another opportunity. Paying it forward

What we do...

We provide access to learner's and driver's license training for youths from disadvantaged communities in order to enhance their chances of employment.

For Who...

The foundation focuses on young people who are 25 years and younger, who either have matric or are in the process of obtaining one.

Our Vision & Mission

To assist in the development of young people from disadvantaged backgrounds by providing access to necessary skills, development and promote education. To provide a vehicle through which young people from disadvantaged backgrounds can be assisted to acquire skills that are necessary for employment seeking.
